## Phi-3 vs same-tier peers (Q2 2024)

| Model | Date | Params | MMLU | HumanEval | On-device? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mistral 7B | 2023-09 | 7B | 60.1% | 30.5% | yes (4-bit quant) |
| Llama 2 7B | 2023-07 | 7B | 45.3% | 12.8% | yes |
| Gemma 7B | 2024-02 | 7B | 64.3% | 32.3% | yes |
| **Phi-3 Mini** | **2024-04** | **3.8B** | **68.8%** | **59.1%** | **yes (smartphone)** |
| Llama 3 8B | 2024-04 | 8B | 66.6% | 62.2% | yes |
| Phi-3 Medium | 2024-04 | 14B | 78.0% | 62.2% | yes (desktop) |

Phi-3 Mini's MMLU at 3.8B exceeds Llama 3 8B at half the parameters — the "data quality" thesis at its strongest single-data-point.
