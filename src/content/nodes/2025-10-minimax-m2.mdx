## What's distinctive

- **10B active / 230B total** — among the lowest active-parameter ratios at frontier benchmark tier
- **Lightning Attention** — the same linear-attention variant pioneered in MiniMax-01; scales to 1M+ tokens cheaply
- **MIT license** — full open-weight, no usage restrictions

## Why it ships fast

Inference cost scales with ACTIVE parameters, not total. M2's 10B active is roughly the cost of running a Llama 3.1 8B, but with 23× more total knowledge to draw from. For agent workflows that make 100+ LLM calls per task, the cost difference compounds.
