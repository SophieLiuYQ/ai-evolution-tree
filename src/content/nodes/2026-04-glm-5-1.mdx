## Notes

GLM-5.1 in non-reasoning mode. Same 744B/40B-active MoE backbone as the
reasoning variant (single set of MIT-licensed weights), just answers
directly instead of producing a reasoning chain. Lower latency, ~7 AA
Intel points lower on the hardest benchmarks.
