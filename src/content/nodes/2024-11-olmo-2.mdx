## What "fully open" means

| Layer | Llama 3 | Qwen 3 | OLMo 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Final weights | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Training code | partial | partial | ✅ |
| Training data | ❌ | partial | ✅ (Dolma) |
| Intermediate checkpoints | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Evaluation harness | ❌ | partial | ✅ |
| Apache / MIT license | ❌ (custom) | mostly | ✅ Apache |

The OLMo / Tulu / Molmo / OLMoE stack is the only complete open-source pipeline at modern frontier scale.
