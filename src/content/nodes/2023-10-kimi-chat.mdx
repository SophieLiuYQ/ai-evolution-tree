## What it is

A consumer chat product (and underlying model) launched October 9, 2023 by Moonshot AI, a Beijing startup founded in March 2023. The differentiating feature: 200,000-token context window at launch, the longest of any Chinese-market LLM and competitive with Anthropic's Claude 2.1 (introduced ~5 weeks later in November 2023).

In March 2024, Moonshot announced expansion to 2 million tokens — the longest publicly-available context window in any LLM at that time, exceeding Gemini 1.5 Pro's 1M (announced one month earlier).

## Use cases that drove adoption

Reports from Chinese-language tech press identify three killer use cases that drove Kimi's 2024 viral spread:

1. **Stock analyst Q&A on full annual reports** — upload 200-page A-share filings, ask "what are the management risks flagged"; Kimi could reason across the entire document rather than retrieving snippets.
2. **Student literature review** — upload 5-10 academic PDFs, ask for a synthesis with citations; competitive with the Western-market Claude 2.1 use case but in Chinese.
3. **Codebase ingestion** — upload an entire small repo (~20 files); useful for coding workflows where developers preferred long-context over RAG-based approaches.

The 2M-token expansion in March 2024 took these use cases to "ingest entire books / mid-sized codebases" scale.
