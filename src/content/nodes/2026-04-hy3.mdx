## Notes

Preview release in Tencent's Hunyuan-3 line. Open weights under the
Tencent HY Community License. Sparse MoE with reasoning post-training,
295B total / 21B active per token, 256K context. AA Intelligence Index
~42 puts it in the open-weights frontier band alongside DeepSeek V3.2.
