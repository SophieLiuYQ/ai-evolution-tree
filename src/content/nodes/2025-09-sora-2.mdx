## What it is

OpenAI's September 30, 2025 Sora successor release. Architecturally undisclosed. Headline new capabilities:

1. **Native audio generation** — synchronized speech, sound effects, ambient audio generated in the same model pass as the visuals (not a separately-cascaded audio model)
2. **Improved spatiotemporal coherence** — physics, object permanence, character identity across cuts substantially more reliable than Sora 1
3. **Consumer app launch** — TikTok-style interface where users see / generate / remix short AI videos
