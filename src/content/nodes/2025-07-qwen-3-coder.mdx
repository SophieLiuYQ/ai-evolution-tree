## Why it matters

Agentic coding requires three things working together:

1. Long context (whole repos, not single files)
2. Tool use (file edits, shell, tests)
3. Multi-turn planning

Qwen3-Coder is the first open model to ship all three competently. It became the de-facto open backbone for self-hosted Copilot replacements within a quarter of release.
