## What it is

A coding-specialized post-training of Qwen 3 base, in two sizes:

- **Qwen3-Coder-480B-A35B** — flagship; matches or beats DeepSeek-V3 on SWE-bench Verified
- **Qwen3-Coder-30B-A3B-Flash** — cheap MoE for IDE autocomplete

Both ship 256K native context, extended to 1M via YaRN scaling. Post-training combined synthetic agent traces, repo-level diffs, and 100+ programming languages.

## Why it matters

Agentic coding requires three things working together:

1. Long context (whole repos, not single files)
2. Tool use (file edits, shell, tests)
3. Multi-turn planning

Qwen3-Coder is the first open model to ship all three competently. It became the de-facto open backbone for self-hosted Copilot replacements within a quarter of release.
