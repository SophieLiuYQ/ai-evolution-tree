## What it shipped

- 389B total / 52B active MoE — at release, the largest open MoE
- Cross-Layer Attention (CLA) for KV-cache compression
- 256K context, training on 7T+ multilingual tokens
- Followed by Hunyuan-Turbo (proprietary), Hunyuan-T1 (reasoning, March 2025), Hunyuan-A13B open MoE (June 2025), and Hunyuan 2.0 (406B/32B active, Dec 2025)
