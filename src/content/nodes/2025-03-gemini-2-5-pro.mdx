## Gemini 2.5 Pro vs frontier peers (Q2 2025)

| Model | Date | Org | AIME | GPQA | SWE-bench V | Context |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| o1 | 2024-09 | OpenAI | 83.3% | 78.0% | — | 128k |
| Claude 3.7 Sonnet | 2025-02 | Anthropic | 80.0% | 84.8% | 70.3% | 200k |
| **Gemini 2.5 Pro** | **2025-03** | **Google DeepMind** | **92.0%** | **84.0%** | **63.8%** | **1M** |
| o3 | 2025-04 | OpenAI | 96.7% | 87.7% | 71.7% | 200k |
| Claude 4 Opus | 2025-05 | Anthropic | — | 83.3% | 79.4% | 200k |

Gemini 2.5 Pro briefly held the AIME lead (March-April 2025) before o3's full release reclaimed it. The 1M-context advantage combined with NotebookLM's research-assistant productization was the durable value.

## Lineage in the Gemini line

| Gen | Date | Headline |
|---|---|---|
| Gemini 1.0 | 2023-12 | tiered family launch |
| Gemini 1.5 Pro | 2024-02 | 1M-token context |
| Gemini 2.0 Flash | 2024-12 | reasoning + agentic upgrades |
| **Gemini 2.5 Pro** | **2025-03** | **native thinking integration** |
| Gemini 3 | 2025-Q4 | next major reset (rumored) |

The shift from "we have reasoning as an experimental feature" to "reasoning is a parameter on the main model" was the productization move that mattered, not the benchmark numbers.
