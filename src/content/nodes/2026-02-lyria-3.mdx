## Lyria family

- **Lyria** (2023) — initial research model, Dream Track integration with YouTube Shorts
- **Lyria 2** (2024) — improved fidelity, long-form support
- **Lyria 3** (Feb 2026) — flagship; integrated in Gemini app
- **Lyria 3 Pro** — higher-fidelity studio variant
- **Lyria RealTime** — interactive / live generative
