- **MatterGen** — Microsoft generative model

- **NVIDIA BioNeMo / MolMIM** — molecular generation
- **Microsoft AI2BMD** — ab initio molecular dynamics
- **Orbital Materials** — startup applying similar techniques to commercial materials discovery
