## LTX-2 spec

- **14B video DiT** — 4K up to 50fps
- **5B audio model** — synchronized sound generation
- **Apache 2.0** — fully unrestricted use
- **LTX Studio** — Lightricks' web tool exposing the model with timeline + multi-clip editing
