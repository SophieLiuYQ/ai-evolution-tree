## What it is

Three foundation models released April 5, 2025:

| Variant | Total params | Active params | Context | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Llama 4 Scout** | 109B | 17B | 10M | longest open context |
| **Llama 4 Maverick** | 400B | 17B | 1M | flagship multimodal |
| **Llama 4 Behemoth** | ~2T (rumored) | ~288B | 256k (preview) | "teacher" for distillation, not released |

Architectural differences vs Llama 3: native Mixture-of-Experts (vs Llama 3's dense-only); native early-fusion multimodal (vision tokens enter the same residual stream as text, vs bolt-on vision encoders); much longer contexts via interleaved RoPE + iRoPE attention variants.
