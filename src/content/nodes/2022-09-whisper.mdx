## How Whisper compares to peers

| System | Year | Languages | Open? | LibriSpeech WER (clean) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Google Speech-to-Text | 2017+ | 125+ | API | ~3% |
| AWS Transcribe | 2017 | ~30 | API | ~5% |
| DeepSpeech 2 | 2015 | 2 (EN, ZH) | open | ~5% |
| wav2vec 2.0 | 2020 | EN+ | open | ~2.5% |
| **Whisper large** | **2022** | **99** | **open** | **2.5%** |
| Whisper large-v3 | 2023 | 99 | open | ~2% |

Whisper's combination of language coverage + open weights + reasonable on-laptop inference (medium variant runs faster than realtime on a Mac) was unique at release.
