## What changed from V3

V3.2 keeps the 671B / 37B active MoE backbone. The new piece is **DeepSeek Sparse Attention (DSA)** — each query attends to a learned subset of keys rather than all of them. At 128K context, attention compute drops ~3-4× with negligible quality loss.

It also bakes "thinking" mode into the base model — no separate R1 distillation step needed.

## Why this matters strategically

DeepSeek has now shipped, in a single year:

1. **V3** — Multi-head Latent Attention + DeepSeekMoE (cheap MoE training)
2. **R1** — Pure-RL reasoning emergence at scale
3. **V3.2** — Sparse attention production deployment

Each is a published recipe. Each changes how the rest of the field builds. Combined, they pushed open-weight Chinese models from "behind" to "ahead on cost" in 12 months.
