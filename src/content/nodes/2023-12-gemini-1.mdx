## What it is

A multimodal foundation model family in three sizes:

| Variant | Params | Deployed |
|---|---|---|
| **Ultra** | undisclosed (~1T+ inferred) | Bard Advanced (Feb 2024) |
| **Pro** | undisclosed | Bard, then Gemini app + Vertex API |
| **Nano-1** | 1.8B | Pixel 8 device (Recorder summarization) |
| **Nano-2** | 3.25B | Pixel 8 Pro device |

Architecturally a single multimodal transformer trained on interleaved sequences of text, image, audio, and video tokens — vs. competitors' bolt-on encoder approaches at the time.

## The contested launch benchmarks

Google's launch announcement claimed Gemini Ultra surpassed GPT-4 on MMLU (90.0% vs GPT-4's 86.4%). Within hours, observers noted:

- Google reported MMLU with a "CoT@32" ensemble (32 independent CoT samples + majority vote) vs GPT-4's standard 5-shot.
- On apples-to-apples 5-shot, Gemini Ultra scored 83.7% — slightly *behind* GPT-4.
- GPT-4 with CoT@32 scored ~87% (per OpenAI subsequent disclosure).

The benchmarking controversy distracted from the actual story: Gemini Ultra was roughly competitive with GPT-4 across most evaluations, with a meaningful native-multimodal advantage and a clear deficit in distribution / product polish.

## Gemini lineage 2023-2026

| Model | Date | Headline change |
|---|---|---|
| Gemini 1.0 (Ultra/Pro/Nano) | 2023-12 | tiered family launch |
| Gemini 1.5 Pro | 2024-02 | 1M-token context (10M experimental) |
| Gemini 1.5 Flash | 2024-05 | fast/cheap variant |
| Gemini 2.0 Flash | 2024-12 | reasoning + native image gen + agentic upgrades |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | 2025-03 | "thinking" mode integrated, top SWE-bench in early 2025 |
| Gemini 3 | 2025-Q4 | next major frontier reset |

The 1M-token context (Gemini 1.5 Pro, Feb 2024) was the most consequential single capability addition in the line — it enabled "video understanding" and "entire-codebase analysis" use cases that competitors couldn't match for ~12 months.
