## What it is

GPT-5 shipped on August 7, 2025. Under the hood it's not one network — it's several specialized subnetworks (fast lane, reasoning lane, multimodal lane) with a trained router that picks one per query. The user never sees which lane fired; they just send a prompt and get an answer.

The point: OpenAI's lineup had bloated into seven user-facing models (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini, o1, o1-pro, o3, o3-mini, o4-mini), each with a narrow task where it was "best." Nobody wanted that in procurement. GPT-5 is OpenAI's Apple-style consolidation — one thing to buy, let the router figure it out.

## GPT-5 vs late-2025 frontier

| Model | Date | Org | Reasoning | Context | Approach |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Claude 4 Opus | 2025-05 | Anthropic | hybrid (toggle) | 200k | per-request thinking flag |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | 2025-03 | Google | integrated | 1M | always-on thinking budget |
| **GPT-5** | **2025-08** | **OpenAI** | **routed** | **400k** | **automatic routing** |
| Llama 4 Maverick | 2025-04 | Meta | none | 1M | non-reasoning open |
| DeepSeek R2 | 2025-Q3 | DeepSeek | dedicated | 128k | open reasoning |

Each frontier lab landed on a slightly different productization choice for "fast vs reasoning":
- **OpenAI**: route automatically (one model surface)
- **Anthropic**: per-request flag (developer chooses)
- **Google**: integrated thinking budget (always reasons, budget controls effort)
- **DeepSeek**: separate models (V3 vs R-series)

The market hasn't converged — different developers prefer different tradeoffs.

## 这是什么

OpenAI 在 2025 年 8 月 7 日放出的「大一统」前沿版本。模型内部由几条专用子网络拼起来（快速通道 / 推理通道 / 多模态通道），上面挂一个学出来的路由器——每收到一个问题就自动选走哪条通道。用户只看到一个「GPT-5」入口，路由是藏起来的。

这是 OpenAI 给 o1 之后产品线混乱问题开的「产品化」药方——以前用户得先知道「X 任务要用 GPT-4o、Y 任务用 o1、Z 任务用 o3」才能用好，现在不用了。

## GPT-5 对比 2025 年末的前沿阵营

| 模型 | 发布 | 厂商 | 推理模式 | 上下文 | 落地方式 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Claude 4 Opus | 2025-05 | Anthropic | 混合（可切换） | 200k | 按请求打开推理标志 |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | 2025-03 | Google | 内建 | 1M | 常开推理 + 预算控制 |
| **GPT-5** | **2025-08** | **OpenAI** | **自动路由** | **400k** | **路由器自行判断** |
| Llama 4 Maverick | 2025-04 | Meta | 无 | 1M | 开源非推理 |
| DeepSeek R2 | 2025-Q3 | DeepSeek | 专用推理模型 | 128k | 开源推理 |

前沿各家在「快速 vs 推理」的产品化路线上走出不同选择：
- **OpenAI**：自动路由（单一模型入口）
- **Anthropic**：按请求标记（让开发者自己决定）
- **Google**：内置推理预算（始终推理，靠预算控制投入）
- **DeepSeek**：分家两套模型（V3 通用 / R 系推理）

市场还没有收敛——不同开发者偏好不同取舍。
