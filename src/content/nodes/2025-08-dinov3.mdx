## What's distinctive

- **No captions required** — pure self-supervised; learns visual structure from raw pixels
- **1.7B image dataset** — largest self-supervised vision pretraining to date
- **Gram anchoring** — novel technique to prevent feature collapse
- **ConvNeXt + ViT variants** — ships as a family, picks architecture per use case
