## What's new

K2 Thinking is built on the existing K2 1T/32B-active MoE base, with three deltas:

1. **Interleaved reasoning + tool calls** — model can pause mid-thought to invoke a tool and resume reasoning with the result.
2. **Native long-horizon RL** — reward signal extends across 200+ step trajectories rather than per-call.
3. **Lower-precision inference** — KAS (Kimi-Aware Sparse) quantization keeps cost down at chain-of-tool depth.

## The $4.6M number

Moonshot reported the post-training run cost ~$4.6M total — about 1/100 of GPT-4-class budgets. The number is debated, but the order of magnitude is supported by their published recipe and similar reports from DeepSeek. The implication: frontier agentic capability no longer requires hyperscaler-class budgets, only the right RL recipe and a base model.
