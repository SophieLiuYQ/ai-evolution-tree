## What "9.3T nucleotides" means

For comparison: a single human genome is ~3 billion base pairs. Evo 2's training corpus is ~3,000 human-genome equivalents drawn from all branches of life — bacteria, archaea, viruses, eukaryotes. The 40B-parameter capacity at 1M context lets the model reason about whole bacterial genomes (typically under 1Mb) end-to-end.
