## What it is

Google DeepMind's third-generation text-to-video model. Architecturally a Diffusion Transformer (DiT) variant with joint audio-video generation — the model produces audio tokens and video latent tokens in coordinated diffusion passes, ensuring synchronization at generation time rather than post-cascading speech-to-video alignment.

## Veo 3 vs Sora 2 — feature parity timeline

| Feature | Veo 3 (May 2025) | Sora 2 (Sep 2025) |
|---|---|---|
| Native audio | ✓ (first) | ✓ (4 months later) |
| Output duration | 8s baseline / longer experimental | 20s |
| Resolution | 4k available (premium) | 1080p baseline |
| Physics quality | strong | strong (slightly better in some cases) |
| Distribution | API + Gemini app | Sora consumer app + ChatGPT |

The competitive dynamic in late 2025 saw Sora and Veo trading capability leads — neither dominating across all dimensions. Real adoption split by use case: professional filmmaking → Veo (via Flow); casual consumer → Sora (via standalone app); Chinese market → Kling.
