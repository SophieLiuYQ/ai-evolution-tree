## What it is

A 104B-parameter decoder-only transformer released open-weight (CC-BY-NC-4.0) on April 4, 2024. Architecturally not novel — Cohere's contributions were in the post-training pipeline:

1. **RAG-optimized fine-tuning** — heavy emphasis on producing accurate citations and integrating retrieved context faithfully
2. **Native tool use** — first-class support for function calling with structured argument schemas
3. **Multilingual coverage** — 10 languages with strong fluency (English, French, Spanish, Italian, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Chinese)
4. **Explicit on-prem deployability** — model card includes detailed compute requirements for self-hosting

## The Cohere strategic position

Cohere is the only major LLM lab founded by Google Brain alumni (Aidan Gomez, an author of "Attention Is All You Need") that explicitly chose enterprise B2B positioning over consumer-AI. The company has stayed relatively small (~300 employees by 2025) and avoided consumer-product launches, focusing exclusively on the B2B funnel. By 2026, this positioning has produced a stable but smaller business than the flagship labs — which Cohere's leadership has framed as deliberate.
