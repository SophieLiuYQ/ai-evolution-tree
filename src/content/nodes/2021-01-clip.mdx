## How it changed downstream architectures

| Year | Model | CLIP role |
|---|---|---|
| 2021 | DALL·E 1 | not used (discrete VAE + transformer) |
| 2022-04 | DALL·E 2 | CLIP text & image embeddings drive diffusion prior |
| 2022-08 | Stable Diffusion | CLIP-L text encoder feeds U-Net cross-attention |
| 2023-07 | SDXL | OpenCLIP-G + CLIP-L dual text encoders |
| 2024-05 | GPT-4o | natively multimodal (CLIP-style pretraining at much larger scale) |

CLIP's scale (400M pairs) was the ceiling-breaker; the architecture was conceptually simple. The data pipeline was the moat.
