## Why Devin mattered

Three things together for the first time:

1. **Long-horizon execution** — multi-hour tasks without human intervention
2. **Multi-tool environment** — shell + browser + editor inside a sandbox
3. **End-to-end product** — not a demo notebook; a hosted agent product

The combination made "agentic coding" a real product category. Subsequent products (Devin successors plus Claude Code, Codex, Cursor Composer, Replit Agent) all share these shapes.
