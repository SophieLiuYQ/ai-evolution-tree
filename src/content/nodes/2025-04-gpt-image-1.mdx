## What's distinctive

- **Native ChatGPT integration** — image gen is just another response type, not a separate tool
- **Reference image input** — pass a photo, ask the model to restyle it, edit it, or extend it
- **Best-in-class text rendering** — readable signs, posters, UI mockups
- **C2PA + watermarking** for commercial trust
