## What it shipped

- 124B total params (123B Mistral Large 2 base + 1B vision encoder)
- Native variable-resolution image input
- Pixtral 12B sibling under Apache 2.0 — smaller, popular for self-hosted vision RAG
- Followed by Mistral's broader push into multimodal across Voxtral (audio) and Magistral (reasoning)
