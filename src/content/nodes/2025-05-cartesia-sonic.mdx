## Why latency matters for voice

In a phone conversation, humans expect responses within ~250ms. Earlier TTS pipelines (200ms TTFA + 200ms LLM + 100ms ASR) blew that budget. Cartesia at 40-90ms TTFA leaves headroom for the LLM to think, keeping the perceived response time under the natural-conversation threshold.
