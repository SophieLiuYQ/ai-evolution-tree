## Why aesthetic still matters

Image generation has bifurcated:

- **Technical accuracy** (prompt adherence, text rendering, anatomy) — led by Reve, Ideogram, GPT Image 1.5, Flux.2
- **Aesthetic quality** (composition, color, mood, "looks designed") — led by Midjourney V7

For commercial design / marketing / concept-art workflows, aesthetic still wins.
