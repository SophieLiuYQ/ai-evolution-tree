## What's new vs Gemini 2.5

- **Native omni-modal** — text, image, video, audio share a unified backbone
- **Deep Think on by default** for hard problems
- **1M-token context** as standard
- **Gemini 3.1 Flash / Flash-Lite** speed/cost tier siblings
- **Image generation** integrated (Nano Banana Pro / Gemini 3 Pro Image)

## The unified-router pattern

Gemini 3 follows the GPT-5 / Claude 4-series pattern: one model name covers a range of behaviors (fast vs deep thinking, single vs tool-use), with the router picking the right mode automatically. The user doesn't choose "reasoning model" anymore; they ask a question.
