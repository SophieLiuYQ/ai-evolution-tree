## The Baker Lab open stack

- **RFdiffusion / RFdiffusion2 / RFdiffusion3** — protein backbone generation
- **RFantibody** — antibody-specialized variant
- **ProteinMPNN** — sequence design from structure
- **LigandMPNN** — ligand-aware sequence design
- **BindCraft** — binder design pipeline
- **RoseTTAFold2 / All-Atom** — structure prediction (parallels AlphaFold)
