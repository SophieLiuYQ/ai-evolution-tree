## What it is

A latent diffusion model for audio generation. The audio is encoded into discrete tokens via EnCodec (Meta's neural audio codec), and a Diffusion Transformer (DiT) denoises the token sequence conditioned on text. Released open-weight under a non-commercial license, with a paid commercial license available from Stability AI.
