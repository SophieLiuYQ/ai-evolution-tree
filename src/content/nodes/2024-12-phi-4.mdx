## What's distinctive

- **14B dense** — small enough to fit on a single A100 in bf16
- **MIT license** — fully permissive for commercial use
- **Synthetic data heavy** — majority of training tokens are LLM-generated and filtered
- **Reasoning focus** — Phi-4-reasoning and Phi-4-reasoning-plus extend with o1-style chain-of-thought training
