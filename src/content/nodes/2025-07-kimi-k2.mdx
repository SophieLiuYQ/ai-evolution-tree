## What it is

A trillion-parameter open-weight Mixture-of-Experts foundation model from Moonshot AI, released July 2025. Architecturally similar to DeepSeek V3 (sparse MoE in the modern style), with Moonshot-specific training data emphasizing Chinese-English bilingual quality and agentic tool use.
