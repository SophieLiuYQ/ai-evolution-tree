## Why it mattered

Three things at once:

1. **Toggleable thinking** — `enable_thinking=True/False` in the chat template switches the same weights between R1-style chain-of-thought and direct answers. No second model.
2. **119 languages** — overtook Llama 3 on multilingual coverage; only Aya Expanse (Cohere) matched it for non-English breadth.
3. **Open-weight reasoning parity** — first non-DeepSeek open release to reach o1-mini / DeepSeek-R1 levels on AIME, LiveCodeBench, and GPQA.

The April 2025 release made it impossible to argue that "real reasoning models are closed-source only."
