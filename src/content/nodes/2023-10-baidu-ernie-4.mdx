## What it is

A closed-API multimodal foundation model released by Baidu on October 17, 2023, two days before Baidu's annual flagship event (Baidu World 2023). Marketed as comparable to GPT-4 on Chinese-language tasks, with native multimodal (image input) capability and integration across Baidu's product surfaces.

## How Ernie compares to peers (late 2023)

| Model | Date | Org | Open? | Chinese strength |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| GPT-4 | 2023-03 | OpenAI | API | strong |
| Claude 2 | 2023-07 | Anthropic | API | weaker |
| ChatGLM3 | 2023-10 | Tsinghua / Zhipu | open | very strong |
| Qwen-72B | 2023-11 | Alibaba | open | very strong |
| **Ernie 4.0** | **2023-10** | **Baidu** | **API** | **strong (claimed)** |
| Yi-34B | 2023-11 | 01.AI | open | strong |

Ernie's closed positioning is the differentiator — among China's flagship LLMs in 2023, Ernie was the most aggressively closed/proprietary, while Qwen, ChatGLM, Yi went open-weight.

## Lineage

- Ernie 1.0 (2019) — pretrained masked LM, predates BERT's wide adoption in some respects
- Ernie 2.0 / 3.0 (2020-21) — knowledge graph integration emphasis
- Ernie 3.5 (2023-06) — Baidu's first chat-tuned consumer release
- **Ernie 4.0 (2023-10)** — flagship multimodal release, this node
- Ernie 4.5 (2024-04) — incremental update
- Ernie X1 (2025) — reasoning variant joining the o1/R1 wave
