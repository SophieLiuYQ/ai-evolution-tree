## What it is

xAI's August 2025 frontier release. Two variants:

- **Grok 4** — single-model flagship in the standard reasoning-with-extended-thinking pattern
- **Grok 4 Heavy** — multi-agent inference: spawns multiple Grok 4 instances reasoning in parallel, synthesizes their outputs into a final answer

Heavy's substantial benchmark lead on the hardest evaluations (Humanity's Last Exam, ARC-AGI v2) comes from this multi-agent inference rather than a different base model.

## The multi-agent inference architecture

Grok 4 Heavy spawns multiple parallel reasoning instances ("agents"), each working on the same problem from a different angle. The instances can share intermediate findings during reasoning. A final synthesis step combines outputs.

Compute cost: ~5-20× a single Grok 4 query. The Heavy tier is priced accordingly ($300/mo consumer subscription, premium API tier).

The architecture echoes earlier "self-consistency" work (Wang et al., 2022) that sampled multiple CoT chains and majority-voted — but goes further by allowing inter-agent communication during reasoning, not just at the answer-synthesis stage.
