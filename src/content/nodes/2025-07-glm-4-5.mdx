## What's distinctive

- **MIT-licensed weights** — most permissive open license at frontier scale
- **Domestic hardware** — GLM-4.6's Cambricon training story (if validated) breaks a key Western talking point
- **Coding + tool use focus** — explicitly trained for agent workflows; WebDev Arena win reflects this
- **Tied to Tsinghua KEG** — academic + commercial dual identity, similar to DeepSeek's role
