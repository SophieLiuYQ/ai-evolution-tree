## What it is

A foundation model family from Alibaba's DAMO Academy / Tongyi Lab, released open-weight in two sizes (7B and 14B) on August 3, 2023, with a 72B variant added in November 2023. Architecturally Llama-2-style with Alibaba-specific tokenizer optimizations for Chinese (vocab ~152k including Chinese characters and common BPE merges).

| ChatGLM2-6B | 2023-06 | 6B | 51.7% | 47.9% | research |
| **Qwen-7B** | **2023-08** | **7B** | **59.6%** | **58.2%** | **commercial OK** |
| **Qwen-14B** | **2023-08** | **14B** | **63.5%** | **66.3%** | **commercial OK** |
| Yi-34B | 2023-11 | 34B | 81.4% | 76.3% | commercial OK |

Qwen-14B was the strongest open Chinese-capable model in summer 2023; Yi-34B briefly took the lead in November but Qwen 1.5 (Feb 2024) and Qwen 2.5 (Sep 2024) reclaimed the position.

## The Qwen lineage (Aug 2023 → 2026)

| Generation | Date | Notable changes |
|---|---|---|
| Qwen 1.0 | 2023-08 | first release, 7B/14B/72B |
| Qwen 1.5 | 2024-02 | sizes 0.5B-72B, including 110B; Apache 2.0 license |
| Qwen 2 | 2024-06 | mixture-of-experts variant (Qwen2-MoE), 128k context |
| Qwen 2.5 | 2024-09 | strong open SOTA across Western and Chinese benchmarks |
| QwQ-32B (preview) | 2024-11 | reasoning model with extended-thinking |
| Qwen 2.5-VL | 2025-01 | multimodal vision flagship |
| Qwen 3 | 2025-Q2 | major refresh, MoE-first family |

By 2025, Qwen models account for an outsized share of model downloads on Hugging Face from non-US users.
