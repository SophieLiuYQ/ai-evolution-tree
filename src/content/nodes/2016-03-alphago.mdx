## What it is

AlphaGo combines:

1. **Policy network** — a deep CNN trained on 30M expert human Go moves to predict the next move
2. **Value network** — a deep CNN trained to evaluate "how good is this board position for the player to move?"
3. **Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS)** — at play time, simulate thousands of plausible games (guided by the policy network) and use the value network to evaluate the resulting positions

The combination beats either component alone. The networks prune the search space (focusing computation on promising moves) and evaluate leaf positions; the search compensates for individual network errors by integrating many evaluations.

## The match

| Date | Game | Winner |
|---|---|---|
| 2016-03-09 | Game 1 | AlphaGo (Lee Sedol resigned at move 186) |
| 2016-03-10 | Game 2 | AlphaGo (move 37 — "alien move," widely studied) |
| 2016-03-12 | Game 3 | AlphaGo |
| 2016-03-13 | Game 4 | Lee Sedol (move 78 — "wedge of God") |
| 2016-03-15 | Game 5 | AlphaGo |

Lee Sedol won the only game any human has ever won against a top AlphaGo variant. After the match, he announced retirement from professional Go in 2019, citing the perceived futility of competing against AI.

## Successors and what they showed

| System | Year | Key change |
|---|---|---|
| AlphaGo Master | 2017-01 | improved version, beat top human players 60-0 in online matches |
| AlphaGo Zero | 2017-10 | learned from self-play only (no human game data); surpassed all prior AlphaGo versions in 40 days |
| AlphaZero | 2017-12 | generalized to chess, Shogi, Go; mastered all three from scratch in ~24 hours |
| MuZero | 2019-11 | learned the rules of the game while playing |
| AlphaFold | 2018, 2020 | applied the architectural lessons to protein structure prediction |
| AlphaStar | 2019 | StarCraft II grandmaster level |
| AlphaCode | 2022 | competitive programming median performance |

The AlphaGo lineage is the most successful applied-AI research arc of the 2010s — and a major part of why DeepMind's status (and Demis Hassabis specifically) is what it is in 2026.
