## What it is

The fourth iteration in the Claude Opus 4.x line, released December 10, 2025. Headline capabilities:

1. **1M-token production context** (with experimental higher contexts)
2. **12+ hour sustained autonomous operation** for agentic tasks
3. **84% SWE-bench Verified** — frontier on real-world software engineering
