## What's distinctive

- 8.3B params (vs Sora's reported 50B+, Wan 2.2's 14B+)
- Single-GPU inference (RTX 4090 / A100 40GB)
- Apache 2.0 license — no usage restrictions
- LoRA fine-tuning support out of the box
